ELPN (English Language Presentation Network) was founded in 2009 by Mr. Rajendiran Prakash with a clear vision — to empower students, teachers, professionals, and job seekers through effective English communication skills.

For more than 17 years, we have been serving various schools and colleges across the states of Tamil Nadu and Kerala, helping thousands of learners improve their spoken English, presentation skills, confidence, and overall personality.
